T-72

Wiki
Full
  • Playstyle
  • About

T-72 manoeuvrability is quite poor, but it makes up for that by being equiped with a AGM. It excels best at disabling/tracking enemy armor that is on the move, that would be hard to hit with AP/HE. The AGMs are unable to outright destroy tanks due to their high health points in most. Firing one AGM to disable, and quickly switching to AP is best course of action when going against enemy armor. The AGMs can also be used against enemy helicopters or IFVs with good effect. IMPORTANT: AGM has min-arm of ~50m and will bounce. Use AP in CQB.
