Leopard

Wiki
Full
  • Playstyle
  • About

Leopard is a challenging tank to gun, its optic requires making a manual zero making it the only tank of its kind for that. The left area on Zoom 3 shows a range card, with an notch. Press X + Scroll to scroll through the ranging. More confusing the range changes based off ammo type. Best to try this vehicle out in the training before using. This tank is best at being static, as engaging targets at constantly changing ranges will overwealm a gunner if they are on the move.


Created in 1970s for the United States Army, it is classified as main battle tank. Seeing use in several conflicts and countries over the last few decades.
