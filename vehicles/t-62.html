T-62 (Updated: v3.1)

Wiki
  • Playstyle
  • About

T-72 maneuverability is quite poor, but it makes up for that by being equipped with an AGM. It's AGM has a min-arm of ~50m and will bounce. AGM is strong against medium health vehicles like LAVs, or as a stealthy opener for tank battles. The T-72 is weaker on paper due to its large ammo rack, and does better in an ambush role.
