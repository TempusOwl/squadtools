FV4034 (Challenger)

Wiki
Full
  • Playstyle
  • About

Challenger is a mid-tier tank with okay maneuverability and speed. It is equipped with RWS loader and commander seat, allowing multiple eyes to see all around the tank for threats while protected from enemy fire. The loader's RWS can kill threats, though its ammo is limited. The main cannon has one of the smallest bullet drop patterns, giving its optics an edge in medium range fights 500-900m to hit the first shot before the enemy does.


Created in 1970s for the United States Army, it is classified as main battle tank. Seeing use in several conflicts and countries over the last few decades.
