Faction Equipment
Squad Leader
The Australian have high-tier SL kits, giving the SL a foregrip-pod + optic that can be used to steady the weapon and dish out accurate hits at range.
General
Each role may have multiple kits under it, by left-clicking will give you the choice of which kit you want to play with. A rifleman for example typically have 2 versions (iron sights or optic). It's always ideal to choose the kit that has an optic, if a kit does not have an optic it has binoculars instead which can be useful due to them having the highest level of zoom.
Squad Kits
The rifleman come equipped with the specter, which is a high tier optic with a good sight picture. Secondly, the medic is essential to any squad. Medics can revive players faster, and they carry bandages. Medics are the are the only method of healing injured players. One medic has an optic, whereas the second does not, in some cases having two medics can be a good idea to sustain pushes/save tickets. The autorifleman is mid-tier with holographic sight.
Fire Support
Anti-Tank crews are equipped with LAWs which are only capable of tracking tanks, or disabling engines on light attack vehicles. The Australian faction has HK417 marksman rifles, it does not have modifiable zero, making it mid-tier.
Specialist
Australian are known best for their specter + machine gun combo that is deadly. The HATs for Australian are mid-tier with simple ranging optic and good damage. They are capable of putting out smoking shells (similar to mortar smokes).
