About Faction
Weapons
The Australian make use of spectre optics for most of their kits, giving them an edge in infantry battles. Their rifles have high rate of fire and moderate recoil that can be managed easily.
Vehicles
The Aussies have mid-tier vehicles, with strong light vehicles. Their ASLAV is an anti-infantry and infantry support IFV, but their AP ammo is scarce. Their PMV, capable of carrying several squad members, make them a flexible/mobile faction. Their tank is a slight downgrade, over the M1A2 with no loader gunner.
Strengths
Their mobility to transport several troops in the PMV across the make can be quite a surprise for the enemy. Using the PMVs effectively gives the AUS an edge over other factions who lack mobility. Weaknesses
They are predictable to play against, having 1 type of IFV and tank. They lack an armor hunter vehicle (IE: Simir Kornet/BPMs AGMs etc..), so they heavily rely on their armor for map control on vehicle heavy maps.
