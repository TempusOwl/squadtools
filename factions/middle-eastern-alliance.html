About Faction
Weapons
Have the highest recoil of any main infantry weapon, thus full auto is never to be used unless within a few meters. Their mags only old 20 rounds, unlike 30 of most factions. Their optic can be challenging for some, others like it (like me). Best thing is the G3 has the highest damage in its class, this translates into having an edge in long range battles where damage drop off becomes an factor.
Vehicles
Have some of the worst vehicles in squad, with largely open top kords and poorly shieled logisitcs simirs. The urals logis are slow, and generally you don't get many of them.
Strengths
Their fast moving Simir Kornet can be quite a surprise for the enemy, not to mention the speed at which their logisitcs simirs and kords can move at.
Weaknesses
Their vehicles are their biggest weakness, relying on intel is important in survival with these otherwise they can easily be attacked by lurking enemies. Often their vehicles will become stranded in the battle.
