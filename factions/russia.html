About Faction
Weapons
The AK has high recoil, making burst firing or tap firing far more important. Spraying can cause you to lose 1v1s. Their optic is an aquired taste, that some may find hard to get use to.
Vehicles
The BTRs are fairly strong, with good fragmention ammo and the ability to transport lots of infantry.
Strengths
Their BMP-2 is quite strong against infantry with tons of frag, while MI-8 carries a hefty amount of supplies to help sustain FOBs.
Weaknesses
Their BTRs lack the amount of health other IFVs have, this really comes into to play most when taking LAT/HATs hits.
