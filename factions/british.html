About Faction
Weapons
Are high tier, with good optics. The recoil of the main rifle is low, allowing for easier control when firing at higher rates.
Vehicles
Their vehicles are lower-tier, having many tracked IFVs. They risk becoming tacked and disabled. They do have secret weapons like the CTAS, which has the best time to kill of all IFVs. Vehicles like the FV107 are a coffin, boasting the worst TKK of 13s for an enemy BTR. Lastly, their MBT has a challenging optic that requires manual adjustment and a fairly exposed ammo rack.
Strengths
Their CTAS is a OP and can destroy vehicles with ease. They have high tier infantry weapons, that will help any squad close the gap.
Weaknesses
Their vehicles are lack luster and require a lot more infantry support. They can't rely on agility and speed to avoid enemy LATs. Their FV107 is awful against OPFOR IFVs, despite being set up to go against them and does not fit in the meta at all.
