Faction Equipment
Squad Leader
The British have high-tier SL kits, giving the SL an foregrip-pod + optic that can be used to steady the weapon and dish out accurate hits at range.
General
Each role may have multiple kits under it, by left-clicking will give you the choice of which kit you want to play with. A rifleman for example typically have 2 versions (iron sights or optic). It's always ideal to choose the kit that has an optic, if a kit does not have an optic it has binoculars instead which can be useful due to them having the highest level of zoom.
Squad Kits
It is always ideal to choose the kit that has an optic, if a kit does not have an optic it has binoculars instead which can be useful if you like spotting on maps like kohat. British LDS optic is high tier, with a good sight picture and ranging marks. By left-clicking the rifleman role at before spawning will present ~3 choices, the top most one has an optic. Secondly, the medic is essential to any squad, medics can revive players faster and they carry bandages, lastly they are the only method of healing injured players. One medic has an optic, whereas the second does not. There is a "marksmen" kit, though it has an L68A2 w/ SUSAT x2 optic, it is simply not worth using over the standard rifle due to how bad the optic is (+ giving up the ammo bag).
Fire Support
Anti-Tank crews are equipped with LAWs which are only capable of tracking tanks, or disabling engines on light attack vehicles. The British faction has sharpshooters rifles for their marksmen and are equipped with 6x optic. Big upside of the British is their fire support machine gun is high-tier using the SUSAT optic, instead of a no optics found in factions like RU.
Specialist
The downside of British is their machine gun has no optic. They make up for this with their HAT having the NLAW which is laser beam of a HAT, requiring no need to range shots.
