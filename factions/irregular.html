About Faction
Weapons
The Australian make use of spectre optics for most of their kits, giving them an edge in infantry battles. Their rifles have high rate of fire and moderate recoil.
Vehicles
PMV can shuttle an entire squad making it a power choice to capture back cap or rotate. The ASLAV carries more ammo than a BTR, edging out against BTRs in battle, though its optic requires learning. M1A1 is the M1A2 (but without a loader MG). MRH carries 1500c giving it an edge over UH-60m.
Strengths
Their optics and slightly better IFVs make them a hard force to fight 1v1.
Weaknesses
They are predictable to play against, having 1 type of IFV and tank. They lack an armor hunter vehicle (IE: Simir Kornet/BPMs AGMs etc..), so they heavily rely on their armor for map control on vehicle heavy maps.
