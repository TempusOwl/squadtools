Squad Mortar Calculator (https://squadmortar.xyz/)

How To Use: Drag the green circle to your mortar location, then double click to create a fire mission. Make sure your mortar type / map is selected from the pull down. The red circle will tell you the range, and azimuth, use this info for your mortar squad/self. You can also display the splash radius, spread amoung other things by clicking the extra settings. Hover over icons to see what each setting does.
