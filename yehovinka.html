Yehorivka

  • Description
  • Playstyle
  • Stats

Based on the eastern european region of Ukraine. Sprawling farm fields with ample trees to cutoff linesights. Map allows you to choose long ranage battles or flank along the trees. It is large map, having FOBs ready is important as running from objective to the next is far from ideal. The map's size encourges vehicle resupply points to be made to cut down on RTB time.
