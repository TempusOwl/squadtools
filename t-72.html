T-72

Wiki
Full
  • Playstyle
  • About

The T-72 is mid-tier tank, suffering from manoeuvrability issues and realtivly large weak spots. The T-72 is typically at a disadvantage on most layers when battling face to face. It makes up for that by being marginally harder kill from the sides (through facing front on is still a must) and its ability to suprise attack with the wire guided AGM that the gunner has access to. AGMs (aka TOWs) are harder to hear/see, allowing you to finish off a disabled tank with your main gun before being discovered. Opening the attack with the AGM makes the T-72 a glass cannon, you can quickly deal damage before the enemy knows what hit them. The T-72 carries only 2 AGMs, thus they should be reserved for large threats like the Abrams or Bradley (because of its own AGM). T-72 is weakest of all (covential faction) tanks for aggresive gameplay due to the weaknesses above. T-72 is best utilized in an ambush/defensive role. The T-72 can be aggresive with its AGM if it is threated, though if you are too close the AGM will not arm itself and bounce making it risk to use within CQB (40m IIRC?). T-72 AGM can hit targets up to 1700 meters away.


Created in 1970s for the United States Army, it is classified as main battle tank. Seeing use in several conflicts and countries over the last few decades.
